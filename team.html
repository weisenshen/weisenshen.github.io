Our Team

Meet the coordinators and participants of USAGE

Workshop Coordinators

The USAGE workshop series is coordinated by the following Principal Investigators:

Weisen Shen

Stony Brook University

Dr. Shen's research focuses on seismic imaging of Earth's structure, with particular emphasis on polar regions. His work combines ambient noise tomography and receiver function analysis to understand crustal and mantle properties beneath ice sheets.

Email: Weisen.Shen@stonybrook.edu

Rick Aster

Colorado State University

Dr. Aster is a seismologist specializing in volcano seismology, glacial seismology, and seismic source processes. He has extensive experience with polar field work and has led numerous Antarctic research projects.

Email: rick.aster@colostate.edu

Mirko Scheinert

TU Dresden, Germany

Dr. Scheinert is a geodesist whose research includes satellite geodesy, gravity field determination, and glacial isostatic adjustment. He is a key contributor to international efforts in Antarctic geodesy and coordinates activities within SCAR-INSTANT.

Email: mirko.scheinert@tu-dresden.de

Community Participants

The USAGE workshops bring together researchers from across the United States and international collaborators. Our community includes seismologists, geodesists, glaciologists, and geophysicists from universities, national laboratories, and research institutions.
