Workshop Overview

The INSTANT II Follow-on Workshop brought together over 40 members of the US polar geophysics community to discuss the future of geodetic-geophysical observational networks in Antarctica and the Arctic. This one-day intensive workshop built upon the foundational discussions from the 2022 Fort Collins meeting and featured in-depth conversations with funding agencies.
