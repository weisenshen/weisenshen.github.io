# 2022 Workshop – Fort Collins, Colorado

**Title:** The Future of Geodetic–Geophysical Observational Networks in Antarctica  
**Date:** September 29 – October 1, 2022  
**Location:** Colorado State University, Fort Collins, CO  

## Overview
This international workshop assessed the status, challenges, and future
directions of geodetic and geophysical observational networks in Antarctica.

## Scientific Themes
- GNSS and geodetic networks
- Seismology and Earth structure
- Glacial isostatic adjustment (GIA)
- Infrastructure, logistics, and coordination
