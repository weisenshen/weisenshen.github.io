# 2024 Follow-on Workshop – Washington Dulles (Hybrid)

**Title:** INSTANT II Follow-on Workshop on the Future of
Geodetic–Geophysical Observational Networks in Antarctica  
**Date:** September 28, 2024  

## Purpose
This workshop built on the 2022 Fort Collins meeting, with emphasis on
community sustainability, coordination models, and future planning.

## Key Topics
- Community-driven observational models
- Facility operations and EarthScope transition
- Training and continuity
- Engagement with NSF and international partners
