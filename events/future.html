Future USAGE Workshop

The USAGE organizing committee is currently planning the next community workshop, tentatively scheduled for late 2026. We are working to identify an optimal location and timing that will maximize community participation.

Topics under consideration:

  • Future of permanent and temporary seismic deployments
  • Integration of new technologies (fiber optic sensing, autonomous systems)
  • Synthesis of recent research findings
  • Early career researcher training opportunities
  • IPY 2032-33 preparation and planning
  • International collaboration frameworks
