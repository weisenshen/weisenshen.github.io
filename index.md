# USAge
## US Antarctica / Arctic Geophysical Explorations

USAge (US Antarctica / Arctic Geophysical Explorations) coordinates and documents
workshops and activities of the US polar geophysics community, with strong alignment
to the SCAR-INSTANT Probing the Solid Earth and its Interactions (PSE) group.

This site serves as a permanent archive of past workshops and as a coordination
point for future community activities, including workshops, meetings, and
collaborative projects related to polar geophysics.
