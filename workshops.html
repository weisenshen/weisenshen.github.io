INSTANT II Follow-on Workshop

This workshop brought together the US polar geophysics community to discuss the future of geodetic-geophysical observational networks. Key topics included future of POLENET, integration with ITGC, data management protocols, and early career opportunities.

The foundational workshop that established the SCAR-INSTANT "Probing the Solid Earth" (PSE) theme. This workshop defined priorities for US polar geophysics research, including grand challenges, network design, and international collaboration frameworks.
