The Future of Geodetic-Geophysical Observational Networks in Antarctica

This workshop brought together the international polar geophysics community to discuss the future of geodetic and geophysical observational networks in Antarctica. The meeting featured six scientific sessions covering observational networks, GNSS applications, seismology, data synthesis, glacial isostatic adjustment (GIA), and infrastructure coordination. The workshop was supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF-OPP), the Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research (SCAR), and Nanometrics Inc.
