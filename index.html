Scientific Mission

USAge coordinates and documents workshops and activities of the US polar geophysics community, with strong alignment to the SCAR-INSTANT Probing the Solid Earth and its Interactions (PSE) group. We serve as a permanent archive of past workshops and as a coordination point for future community activities.
